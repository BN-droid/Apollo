Diet: Adolescents should follow a balanced diet that includes fruits and vegetables, lean proteins (chicken, fish, tofu, legumes), whole grains, and dairy products. They should avoid excessive consumption of fast food and sugary beverages. It's crucial to ensure they get enough calcium and iron, essential for growth and development.

Physical Activity: Teens should engage in at least 60 minutes of physical activity each day. Recommended activities include jogging, swimming, dancing, team sports like soccer and basketball, and outdoor activities like hiking and climbing.

Gym: Teens can benefit from gym workouts, provided they are supervised by a qualified instructor. They can do light weightlifting, participate in fitness classes, and use gym machines to improve strength and endurance.

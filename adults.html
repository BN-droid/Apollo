Diet: Adults should follow a diet rich in vegetables, fruits, lean proteins (chicken, fish, legumes, tofu), whole grains, and healthy fats (such as those found in avocados, nuts, and olive oil). It's advisable to moderate the consumption of alcohol and processed foods. Ensure an adequate intake of fiber, vitamins, and minerals.

Physical Activity: Adults should aim for at least 150 minutes of moderate aerobic activity or 75 minutes of vigorous activity per week, plus muscle-strengthening exercises twice a week. Recommended activities include brisk walking, cycling, yoga, pilates, weightlifting, and participating in fitness classes.

Gym: The gym is a great option for following a structured training program. Adults can use free weights, exercise machines, do cardio, and participate in group classes to improve strength, endurance, and flexibility.
